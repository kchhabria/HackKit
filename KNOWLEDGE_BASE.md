## Judging

### Format
- Teams present for 7 minutes total (including Q&A from judges)
- Scoring: 1-10 per category (whole numbers)
- Weighted totals calculated automatically

### Scoring Categories

| Category | Weight | Criteria |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Business Impact / Industry Alignment | 40% | Working Backwards document quality, problem/solution clarity, presentation effectiveness |
| Technical Excellence | 40% | PartyRock feature utilization, implementation quality, working demonstration |
| Innovation | 20% | Unique approach, creative solution, original thinking |

### Detailed Rubric (1-4 scale per criterion, mapped to 10-point total)

**Business Impact (40%)**
- Excellent (4): Excellent PRFAQ with clear value prop, identifies significant real-world problem, engaging professional delivery with excellent visual aids
- Good (3): Well-written PRFAQ with good business rationale, well-organized presentation with good communication skills
- Satisfactory (2): Adequate PRFAQ with problem identified but solution alignment could be stronger, some unclear elements
- Needs Improvement (1): Poor PRFAQ with vague problem definition or poor solution fit, difficult to follow

**Technical Excellence (40%)**
- Excellent (4): Demonstrates advanced PartyRock features with sophisticated AI integration
- Good (3): Good use of PartyRock capabilities with solid technical execution
- Satisfactory (2): Basic PartyRock implementation that functions correctly
- Needs Improvement (1): Minimal technical complexity, basic functionality only

**Innovation (20%)**
- Excellent (4): Highly original AI solution addressing a unique problem with creative approach
- Good (3): Original solution with some creative elements and clear innovation
- Satisfactory (2): Some creative elements but follows common approaches
- Needs Improvement (1): Limited creativity, follows common approaches

### Scoring Scale Summary
- Excellent: 10-12 points (outstanding, top recognition)
- Good: 7-9 points (strong with minor areas for improvement)
- Satisfactory: 4-6 points (meets expectations)
- Needs Improvement: 0-3 points (below expectations)
