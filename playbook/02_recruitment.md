# 02 - Student Recruitment (6-4 Weeks Out)

## Objective
Fill 60 seats with engaged students. No coding experience required.

## Step-by-Step

### 1. Create the Flyer
- [ ] Use template: `templates/flyer_template.md`
- [ ] Customize: university name, date, time, venue, QR code to registration
- [ ] Key selling points to emphasize:
  - Free lunch
  - No coding required
  - Portfolio-ready project for resume
  - Network with AWS professionals
  - Limited spots (creates urgency)

### 2. Distribution Channels
- [ ] University faculty (ask them to share with students)
- [ ] CS/Engineering department mailing lists
- [ ] Student clubs (CS club, AI club, entrepreneurship club)
- [ ] University career center
- [ ] LinkedIn (tag university, use relevant hashtags)
- [ ] Physical flyers on campus (if allowed)

### 3. Registration Management
- [ ] Monitor sign-ups weekly
- [ ] Send confirmation email to registrants
- [ ] At 50 registrants: activate waitlist
- [ ] At 2 weeks out: send reminder email with logistics
- [ ] At 1 week out: final reminder with what to bring (laptop, charger)

### 4. Expect ~50% Show Rate
- [ ] If you want 60 attendees, register 80-100
- [ ] Plan for 30-40 as realistic attendance
- [ ] This is normal for free university events

## Deliverables
- [ ] Flyer created and distributed
- [ ] Registration form getting sign-ups
- [ ] Confirmation emails sent
- [ ] Headcount estimate for catering
