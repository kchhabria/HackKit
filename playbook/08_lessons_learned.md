# 08 - Lessons Learned

## From UMD 2026 (April 24, 2026)

### What Worked Well
- Working Backwards methodology resonated strongly with students
- PartyRock no-code approach meant ALL students could participate
- 7-minute presentation format kept energy high
- Having AWS alumni from the university built instant credibility
- "Portfolio-ready deliverable" framing motivated students
- Lunch career panel was informal and well-received

### What to Improve Next Time
- Start registration earlier (6+ weeks out)
- Over-register by 50-80% to account for no-shows
- Test PartyRock on university WiFi in advance
- Have mobile hotspot backup if WiFi fails
- Consider assigning teams rather than self-selection
- Print rubric for judges as physical backup

### Tips for Future Organizers
- Friday 10am-3pm is the sweet spot
- Free lunch is the #1 draw for students
- Keep the WB presentation under 30 min
- Floating mentors are essential during build time
- Announce "pencils down" 15 min before presentations
- Take photos throughout for the LinkedIn post
