# 04 - AWS Team Preparation (2-1 Weeks Out)

## Objective
Brief all AWS volunteers so everyone knows their role on event day.

## Role Assignments

| Role | Count | Responsibilities |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| Event Lead | 1 | MC, keeps time, manages flow, troubleshoots |
| Segment Lead | 1 | Records team names, assigns judges, manages scoring app |
| Presenter (WB) | 1-2 | Delivers Working Backwards presentation |
| Presenter (PartyRock) | 1 | Demos PartyRock, runs hands-on workshop |
| Floating Mentors | 2-4 | Help teams during build time, answer questions |
| Judges | 3-4 | Score presentations (can overlap with other roles) |
| Career Panel | 2-3 | Speak during lunch about AWS careers |

## Step-by-Step

### 1. Send Volunteer Briefing
- [ ] Use template: `templates/volunteer_briefing.md`
- [ ] Include: date, time, venue address, parking info, agenda
- [ ] Assign roles from table above

### 2. Judge Preparation
- [ ] Share the rubric with all judges (see `06_judging.md`)
- [ ] Confirm judges understand the 3 categories and weights
- [ ] Remind judges: 7 minutes per team, ask at least 1 question

### 3. Logistics Confirmation
- [ ] Confirm catering order (dietary restrictions from registration)
- [ ] Confirm AV setup (projector, mic, adapters)
- [ ] Prepare name tags or sign-in sheet
- [ ] Print: agenda handouts, rubric for judges, team number cards
- [ ] Bring: power strips, HDMI adapters, markers, sticky notes

### 4. Dry Run (Recommended)
- [ ] 30-min virtual call with all volunteers
- [ ] Walk through the agenda
- [ ] Confirm who presents what
- [ ] Answer questions

## Deliverables
- [ ] All volunteers briefed and confirmed
- [ ] Roles assigned
- [ ] Logistics confirmed
- [ ] Judges understand rubric
