# 01 - Planning (8-6 Weeks Out)

## Objective
Lock in the fundamentals: university, date, venue, budget, and core team.

## Step-by-Step

### 1. Identify Your University Partner
- [ ] Reach out to a faculty contact (CS/Engineering department preferred)
- [ ] Use template: `templates/email_invite_professor.md`
- [ ] Confirm they can provide: venue, student outreach channels, AV support
- [ ] Get faculty sponsor name for flyers/communications

### 2. Secure Budget
- [ ] Estimate costs:
  - Catering (lunch for 60-70 people): $1,500-2,500
  - Swag/prizes (optional): $500-1,000
  - Printing (flyers, name tags): $100-200
  - Misc (supplies, adapters): $200
- [ ] Submit budget request through your org's process
- [ ] Get approval in writing

### 3. Book the Venue
- [ ] Requirements:
  - Capacity: 60+ students + 8-10 AWS staff
  - WiFi: reliable, supports 70+ devices
  - Power: outlets or power strips for laptops
  - AV: projector + screen, microphone (if large room)
  - Layout: tables for teams of 4-6, plus a presentation area
  - Catering-friendly: space for food setup
- [ ] Confirm date and time (recommended: Friday, 10am-3pm)
- [ ] Get room confirmation in writing

### 4. Assemble Your AWS Team
- [ ] You need:
  - 1 Event Lead (you, the person running this playbook)
  - 1 Segment Lead (manages judging logistics on the day)
  - 4-6 Mentors/Judges (mix of CSMs, SAs, leaders)
  - 1-2 Account Team contacts (for university relationship)
- [ ] Send calendar holds to all volunteers
- [ ] Use template: `templates/volunteer_briefing.md`

### 5. Set Up Registration
- [ ] Create registration form (Smartsheet or Google Form)
- [ ] Fields: Name, Email, Major, Year, Dietary Restrictions, T-shirt Size
- [ ] Set capacity cap (recommended: 60)
- [ ] Create waitlist overflow

## Deliverables at End of This Phase
- [ ] Confirmed date and venue
- [ ] Approved budget
- [ ] 4-6 AWS volunteers committed
- [ ] Registration form live
- [ ] Faculty contact confirmed
